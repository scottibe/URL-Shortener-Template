Rigorous, Industry-Aligned Curriculum

On campus or online, Flatiron students receive an uncompromising education in programming, with rigorous but rewarding coursework that simulates the challenging responsibilities of a real-world developer. Since our inaugural class in 2012, we have continually honed our curricula to graduate engineers who are ready to hit the ground running at top employers like Google, ESPN, NASA and Spotify.
